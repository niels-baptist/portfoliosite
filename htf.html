Hack the future 2021

On the third of December last year (2021), my friend and fellow Thomas More student Pieter Pauwels - who studies Application Development in the 3rd year - decided to form a pair of 2 and participate in the Hack The Future Hackathon event. We chose for the sharepoint challenge since we were already a bit acquainted with this and since it seemed like a good fir for both of our qualities. From the morning up until the evening, we had a lot of fun while tackling a crime-themed challenge, in which we had to develop a web and mobile application that would allow a detective to intelligently share information with his/her team. For this, we used Microsoft sharepoint and powerapps.

We - our teamname being Crimicloud - ended up winning the challenge and taking home a Logitech Brio Webcam as our price, but the most important thing we gained from this, was an amazing experience.
